Keep Growing with JCCC Continuing Education

JCCC Continuing Education brings you dynamic opportunities to enhance career development and deepen personal enrichment. You'll find many opportunities to expand your skills or build aknowledge base for a new career. Have fun exploring a new hobby and stretch your brain with life and leisure courses. You're sure to find exactly what you need and maybe the unexpected when scrolling through upcoming Continuing Education course offerings.

We've Got What You Need!

Seeking new workplace skills, professional development or on a quest for personal enrichment? We have courses and certificates to help you move forward. Industry experts and caring instructors teach engaging and innovative training at the time and place you want to learn. They will respond to your questions or concerns and encourage you to succeed.

If you're ready to retool your skill set, we offer Career Training Certificates and prep courses for Industry Certifications. When you complete your training, you'll be ready to apply for professional-level positions in high-demand occupations — healthcare, IT, marketing, management, process improvement or a variety of trades.

Our registration staff is ready to help you find a class that's right for you! Contact us at CERegistration@jccc.edu or call 913-469-2323. We look forward to serving you!

Customized Training

Enjoy training programs tailored to meet the needs of your business or organization. Allow us to align instruction with your organization’s needs and values. Then, relax while we manage logistics and deliver customized training that works for you. For more information call 913-469-2323.

What’s an EDTalk?

Glad you asked! EdTalks are a FREE weekly lecture or presentation on a mixed bag of topics — everything from Storm Chasing to How to Keep Mentally Strong. Join a subject matter expert via Zoom for 45 minutes of learning and inspiration. There’s always time for Q&A, too. You canview upcoming EDTalk topics and register to attend at jccc.edu/edtalks.

Course Delivery Formats

We’ve got the training you and your team need at the time and place you want it. Whether it’s instructor-led or on-demand, public courses or private training, JCCC has a delivery option to match your preferences.

In-person Training Courses

Participate in rich discussions in a traditional classroom setting on campus

Live Online Courses

Engage in courses remotely via your computer, laptop or other device

Log in to the actual classroom using Zoom and virtually attend live online

Participate in real-time discussions with your instructor and fellow students via technology

Receive course access links within 2 business days of the course start date

Online Courses

Select on-demand learning that allows you to develop skills and work at your own pace

Access your course at any time — day or night — as noted in the description

Learn using videos, reading and knowledge checks

WHAT TECHNOLOGY DO I NEED TO BE SUCCESSFUL IN AN ONLINE CE COURSE? You will need access to a:

Reliable computer

Reliable Internet connection (preferably high speed)

Web camera with a microphone (required only for live online courses)



Some courses may have additional requirements. Please review recommendations in the course listing before you register and reach out to Registration at ceregistration@jccc.edu if you have questions.

Issues with registering?, please contact us at CERegistration@jccc.edu or call 913-469-2323.
